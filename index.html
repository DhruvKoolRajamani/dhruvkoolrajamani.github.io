My interests lie in the field of non-linear control and Robot dynamics. To cultivate my interest, I am researching on the control of a flexible manipulator using differential flatness. I am a robotics enthusiast and wish to pursue further studies to better develop my interests and understanding of robots.

Underactuated Flexible Manipulator

Designed a 3-link and 4-link planar manipulator on MATLAB and implemented a nonlinear control theory called Differential Flatness, allowing the end effector on the manipulator to follow a desired trajectory with just 2 non-colinear input forces.

What's Next?
I hope to extend this theory to n-links and actually make a physical robot to validate my simulations.

Mars Rover

Developed a Mars Rover prototype that flaunts a modified rocker-bogie suspension, low pressure balloon tires, custom designed scientific testing mechanism, 6-DOF Robotic Arm and can run autonomously for as long as an hour.

Robotic Arm Lead

Other than developing the entire Rover, I was in charge of designing a 6-DOF Robotic Arm that fit the competition criteria with added features such as a self-adapting parallel jawed gripper and a modular assembly (for easy replacement of parts).
