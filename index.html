About

Previous Next

I've been tinkering with robots since Middle School with the advent of the LEGO Mindstorms robot. Supplementing my LEGO building skills with basic functional programming tools I began experimenting with microcontrollers through DIY Projects available online.

Fast forward to college, I continued expanding my interest in Robotics by joining a budding Robotics Team - Mars Rover Manipal and by the end of my first year, the team and I had designed our very own Mars Rover Prototype.

I cultivated my interest of Biomechanics through my internship at the BioRobotics Lab at EPFL where I was exposed to a plethora of bioligically inspired Robots. Inspired by the projects undertaken by the scholars at the Lab, I've co-founded a group in my University that is accessible to all like minded scholars. Being the first of it's kind, the Biorobotics Group is a community of scholars sharing their research and ideas, as well as a platform to teach others our knowledge of Robotics.

Enough about me, come take a look at my Robots.
